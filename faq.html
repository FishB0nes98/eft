About EFT Tapping

What is EFT Tapping and how does it work?

EFT Tapping, or Emotional Freedom Technique, is like giving your emotions a gentle nudge towards peace. You tap on specific points on your body while focusing on the issue you want to release. It's like a mini acupuncture session without the needles, helping to balance your energy and calm your mind. EFT Tapping has been shown to reduce stress, anxiety, and even physical pain by lowering cortisol levels and promoting relaxation, as well as performance enhancement. As Gary Craig says: "EFT, by far, is the most fascinating, most rewarding, most effective tool I have ever found for personal improvement. Try it on anything!"

Can EFT Tapping really help with anxiety and stress?

You bet! EFT Tapping has been known to work wonders on anxiety and stress. It's like giving your worries a friendly tap-out, helping you find some much-needed inner chill. Many folks have found it to be a game-changer for managing those pesky negative emotions. Studies have shown that EFT can lead to a significant decrease in anxiety levels.

What are some common misconceptions about EFT Tapping?

One common misconception is that EFT Tapping is just a placebo. However, over 300 studies suggest that EFT Tapping is more than just a placebo, providing relief from a variety of concerns and boosting mental, emotional, and physical well-being. Another misconception is that it requires a lot of time and practice to see results, but many people experience benefits even after their first session.

How can I prepare for an EFT Tapping session?

It's helpful to have a quiet space, some tissues and water nearby, and a pen and paper to jot down any insights or emotions that arise, although I will do the writing, so you just need to focus on yourself. Make sure before the session that your device is charged and the lighting is good, as I need to see you properly to help you through your process. Remember, there's no need to have expectations about how the session should go; easing into the process often leads to the best results.

What should I expect during an EFT Tapping session?

During an EFT Tapping session, you'll be guided to focus on a particular issue while tapping on specific points on your body. It's common to experience a range of emotions, and some people may feel a sense of release or relief. I will always tell you everything and guide you through the process. No need to worry. Easy peasy!

Can EFT Tapping be used for physical ailments as well?

Absolutely! EFT Tapping can be effective for a variety of physical ailments, including headaches, chronic fatigue, and even autoimmune conditions. By addressing the emotional components that may contribute to these issues, EFT can help restore balance and promote healing.

How do I know if EFT Tapping is right for me?

EFT Tapping is suitable for anyone looking to manage stress, anxiety, or emotional challenges. It's a non-invasive technique that can be easily integrated into your daily routine. I will teach you how. Many people find that EFT Tapping provides them with a sense of empowerment and control over their emotions.

What is the recommended session frequency?

You might just need one session to put something into place in your life. If you are more committed for "fast lane" results, I recommend at least two sessions per week. However, everyone is unique, and so is their healing journey. Some clients prefer to sit with it for a while after a session, while others prefer more frequent sessions, even daily, for a more intensive experience. We'll discuss the ideal frequency for you, tailoring our approach to your specific needs and goals.

Spiritual and Holistic Techniques I Use

What is Ho'oponopono and how can it help me?

Ho'oponopono is a Hawaiian practice that's all about forgiveness and letting go. It's like giving your inner critic a timeout. By repeating phrases like "I love you, I'm sorry, please forgive me, thank you," you can release old baggage and find some inner peace. This practice can help you forgive yourself and others, leading to a sense of mental and emotional cleansing.

How does Angel Card reading work?

Angel Card reading is like having a chat with your guardian angels. You shuffle a deck of cards, pick one, and let the message guide you. It's a fun way to get some spiritual insights and a nudge in the right direction. Think of it as a cosmic pep talk.

What is Byron Katie's "The Work" technique?

Byron Katie's "The Work" is a bit like a truth-seeking mission for your thoughts. You question your beliefs and assumptions to see if they're really true. It's a bit like being your own detective, but for your mind. This technique helps you identify and challenge the thoughts that cause suffering, leading to more peace and freedom.

Can meditation really make a difference in my life?

Absolutely! Meditation is like a spa day for your mind. It can help you feel more centered, reduce stress, and even boost your creativity. It's a bit like hitting the reset button on your brain. Meditation has been shown to create large and rapid drops in anxiety, depression, and other psychological symptoms.

What are Access Bars and how do they work?

Access Bars are like energy highways on your head. By gently touching these points, you can release stored energy and old patterns. It's a bit like giving your brain a gentle massage. This therapy can help you clear out mental and emotional blocks, allowing for a fresh start.

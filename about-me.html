About Me

My Journey to Healing

Ever felt like you're walking around with a heart that's like a marshmallow in a microwave - squishy, sensitive, and ready to explode with feelings at the slightest touch? That was me! It's like my heart has a direct line to every emotional rollercoaster out there. Everything touches my heart, and I've always had this overwhelming desire to help everyone around me.

From Dreams to Reality

Childhood Dreams

Growing up, I dreamed of being a doctor so I could heal my mom forever. However, life had other plans, and I ended up becoming a hairdresser!

Creative Passion

From healing hands to styling hands. I loved the chaos of the salon, the energy, and the chance to be creative. Drawing, crocheting, and DIY projects have always been my passions.

New Purpose

Now I've found a way to pour my creative energies into something new: making jewelry from natural materials and helping people heal.

The Salon Life Revelation

The salon life had its downsides. People would come in, complain about their lives, and I would think to myself, "Okay, let's fix this!" However, they just wanted someone to listen and fix their hair, not their problems. It was exhausting, and I felt helpless. I quit, even though I was told I was very good at hairstyling.

My perspective has changed. Who would have thought that I, the girl who left hairdressing because she was constantly hearing about other people's problems would become someone who listens to people's problems all the time? Yes, I am here because I found tools to help people who want to help themselves. I have a goal. I want to help others, just as someone once helped me.

My Transformation Journey

A decade ago, I was a hot mess. Then, I discovered some amazing techniques that helped me to overcome traumas and the stories I told myself that kept me stuck. For so long, I thought that life was just a struggle - and that that was all I had. As a result of applying these techniques, I slowly started to change and my life changed around me.

The Breakthrough Moment

I remember the moment in the kitchen when a friend's behavior unexpectedly triggered a deep, childhood wound. I was overwhelmed and retreated to the bathroom to be alone with my tears. I let myself feel the pain fully. Intense sobs came from a childlike voice. I collapsed onto the floor, pounding my chest with my fists.

I spoke the heartache out loud, the heartache that the child that was once me had felt: "I am being rejected again. Love is being taken from me. I am misunderstood, trapped by invisible rules." I used tapping and pulled myself out of a panic attack. This was when I discovered how powerful EFT is.

Finding My Path to Help Others

I have found my path to healing and now I want to share it with the world. There is a way to deal with trauma, a simple way that anyone can learn. I have used it to turn my life around. There is now joy in my life where once there was nothing more positive than a fleeting happiness.

You can learn the way too! You do not have to do it alone. I can be there to help you. Let's face whatever is holding you back together. By recognizing and working on your traumas, you can transform your reality. So, don't be shy - reach out, and let's start this journey together. I'm here to help, one tear and one laugh at a time!

Ready to Start Your Journey?

If my story resonates with you, and you're ready to begin your own transformation, I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

With love and healing,

Chilly
